CompanyIntroduction

Zamil Trade & Services

Zamil Trade & Services was established in 1920. It is a leading industrial and commercial organization known for its diverse expertise and adherence to international standards.
Over the years, we have built a strong global presence, providing premium products and value-added services to millions​ of customers worldwide.
With over a century of experience, we have consistently delivered superior quality​ and innovative solutions.
Our dedication to customer satisfaction and excellence allows us to compete with global industry leaders, continuously expanding and evolving to meet the demands of an ever-changing market.

Our Vision

To be the Market Leader of Choice in various segments by catering to providing our products and services worldwide.

Our Mission

We function as Sales Agents / Sponsors to overseas manufacturers to provide our customers with Premium Quality Engineering Products, Services, and Supply Chain Services & Solutions with the Best Prices.​
